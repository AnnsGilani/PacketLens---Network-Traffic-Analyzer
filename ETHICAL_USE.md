# Ethical Use Notice

PacketLens is intended for education, defensive security, authorized monitoring, and analysis of traffic on systems or networks you own or are explicitly permitted to inspect.

Do not use PacketLens to:

- Monitor another person's traffic without permission
- Capture credentials or private data
- Scan or probe third-party systems without authorization
- Bypass security controls
- Disrupt networks or services

The user is responsible for complying with applicable laws, organizational policies, and privacy requirements.

PacketLens alerts are heuristic indicators. They should be investigated before being classified as confirmed malicious activity.
